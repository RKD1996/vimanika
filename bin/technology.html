Our Product's Features

VTOL

(Vertical Take Off and Landing) gives the ability to takeoff and land on building rooftops or small land area , means no runway required .

Electric

100% Electric : It uses rechargeable Li ion battery system which ensure zero carbon emission.

Auto-Pilot

Uses complete autonomous flight controller and provides one click destination select, which have triple failsafe feature.

Safety Systems

Redundant propulsion that ensure safety in case of failure in one of rotor. Ballistic parachute: To ensure ultimate safety of people on board, it is equipped with full body ballistic parachute which can provide safe lending in case of complete failure.

Wing Based Design

To improve cruise efficiency , safety in terms of more glide , we are using tilt wing design.
