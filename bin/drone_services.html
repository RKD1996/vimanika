Drone Services

To Make a Better Wrold


Product Features At a Glance:-

Agriculture Survey

Drone that uses spectrum sensor to scan crops. Data collected used to generate vegetation index map that can tell health of crops/plants etc. with exact geo tag. This critical data can be used to target the unhealthy crop directly rather than using fertilizers on complete area.

Cargo delivery

We have custom-made drone which has heavy lift capacity or payload delivery up to 40 KG. Possible use cases in off road terrain where payload delivery is very critical like in hilly areas.

Land/Mining Survey

A high altitude drone can be very useful and time saving when it can cover a large area in one flight. Using high definition camera images fed into customized software results a 3 D mapping of land area .

LiDAR Support

LiDAR (light, detection and Ranging) is a break-through in technology and we are using it to help government department to collect exact data and repair the broken roads. Various other use cases are also possible in infrastructure survey.

Surveillance

High definition camera equipped drone allow surveillance on the coastal areas/ defense zone or any area from where you want to monitor live feeds. It can provide live feed from range of 7 KM.
